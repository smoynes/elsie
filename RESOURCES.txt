 Resources
===========

The authors of these resources are true heroes.

- Student resources for second edition. There are solution sets and appendixes
  that are useful, even if for an old edition with a few significant
  differences:
  https://highered.mheducation.com/sites/0072467509/

- Publisher's simulator updated for the third edition:
  https://github.com/chiragsakhuja/lc3tools

- A really fantastic web-based LC-3 simulator and assembler:
  https://wchargin.com/lc3web/

- A complete LC-3 development and learning toolset:
  https://github.com/TricksterGuy/complx-tools

- Lecture notes on the ISA:
  https://www.cs.utexas.edu/users/fussell/courses/cs310h/lectures/Lecture_10-310h.pdf

- Post-apocalyptic programming:
  https://zserge.com/posts/post-apocalyptic-programming/
